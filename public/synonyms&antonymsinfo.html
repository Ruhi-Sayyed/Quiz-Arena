Synonyms and Antonyms

Enhance your vocabulary by understanding words with similar and opposite meanings.

What Are Synonyms?

Synonyms are words that have the same or nearly the same meaning as another word.

  • Happy – Joyful, Cheerful, Content
  • Fast – Quick, Rapid, Swift
  • Smart – Intelligent, Bright, Clever

What Are Antonyms?

Antonyms are words that have opposite meanings.

  • Hot – Cold
  • Light – Heavy
  • Strong – Weak

Examples in Sentences:

  • Happy – She felt happy after hearing the good news.
  • Fast – The cheetah is a fast animal.
  • Smart – He gave a smart answer during the quiz.
