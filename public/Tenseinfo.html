Understanding Tenses

Master verb tenses to speak and write with accuracy.

What are Tenses?

Tenses show the time of action or state of being. There are three main tenses: Present, Past, and Future.

Examples:

  • Present Simple: She reads books every day.
  • Past Simple: He played football yesterday.
  • Future Simple: They will travel next week.
  • Present Continuous: I am watching a movie.
  • Past Perfect: She had finished her work.

Tips:

  • Use helping verbs for continuous and perfect tenses.
  • Keep subject-verb agreement in mind.
