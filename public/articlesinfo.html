Articles in English

Learn how to use 'a', 'an', and 'the' correctly in sentences.

What Are Articles?

Articles are words used before nouns. There are two types:

  • Indefinite: a, an
  • Definite: the

Usage:

  • a → Used before consonant sounds (e.g., a pen, a university)
  • an → Used before vowel sounds (e.g., an apple, an hour)
  • the → Used for something specific or previously mentioned (e.g., the sun, the book on the table)

Examples:

  • I saw a dog.
  • She ate an ice cream.
  • The moon looks beautiful tonight.
  • A unique idea. (“unique” sounds like “you”)
